Banana Split

Shaped with vanilla, chocolate and strawberry ice cream in a row. Smothered with crushed red maraschino cherries, crushed pineapple, real strawberries, surrounded by bananas and topped with a mound of whipped cream and a cherry!

Sundae

Sundaes are fashioned with vanilla ice cream, with your choice of toppings that include Caramal, Marshmallow, Pineapple, Strawberry, Cherry, Peanut Butter, Chocolate Syrup, Vanilla Syrup or Hot Fudge (seasonal toppings available), topped off with a mountain of whipped cream.

Smoothie

Consist of our delicious sherbet blended with fruit and topped off with whipped cream. Flavors include Strawberry Banana, Strawberry Razberry, Pina-Coolada, plus a special flavor of the month.

Waffle

We start with a crunchy vanilla waffle cone that's baked to perfection right in our store. Then you build your masterpiece by choosing from more than 24 fresh flavors of ice cream loaded with yummy ingredients. If you're feeling particularly decadent, add any of our fabulous toppings to make it extra special.

Icecream pops

A generous scoop of Joe's homemade ice cream covered in our rich, dark, gourmet chocolate served on a stick! Handel's serves four flavors at all times: vanilla, chocolcate, mint chocolate chip and a special flavor of the month.

Icecream Cake

Our two layer cakes consist of a chocolate or yellow cake on the bottom, a thick layer of deep dark chocolate fudge in the center, and your favorite ice cream or yogurt on the top. Serves 14-16. Our one layer cakes consist of your favorite ice cream or yogurt with an optional layer of chocolate crunchies in the center.
