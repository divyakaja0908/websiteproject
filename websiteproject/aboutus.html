About Joe's Ice Cream

History

Joe's Homemade Ice Cream burst onto the ice cream scene like a Chocolate Gusher in March of 1975. It was started by a fellow by the name of Joe Freund, an ice cream salesman, a retail businesses operator, and a teacher at Roxbury High School. Knowing what people really wanted, Joe developed his own, homemade, high-quality ice cream with original flavors. Since opening, Joe's has stayed relatively the same, including their nostalgic walk-up counter. Over the years, Joe's has been quietly winning award after award in national competitions. In 2003, Joe's won first place for Vanilla and Strawberry flavors in a national ice cream retailers contest. They won second place for Sticky Bun ice cream in 2004 and took both first and second place in 2005 for Desert Silk and Graham Central Station. In 2006, they won second and third place for their Peach Melba and Holy Cannoli flavors.
